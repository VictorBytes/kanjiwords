Kanji Frequency Dictionary

Find common words containing Kanji

This app displays the words containing the character (usually Kanji) provided as input. It searches through a list of the 14,768 most common Japanese words.

Below are links to search some very common Kanji, or you can use the search bar on top right to input your own.

Search for 人 Search for 日 Search for 学 Search for 明
This app hopefully provides all the words that you will ever need to know for a particular Kanji, in order to be fluent. You can prioritize the most common ones first, as they appear in descending frequency order.
Column "Ranking" of the resulted table indicates the position in the top 14,768 most common Japanese words. The source of the ranking is the list created by the University of Leeds. The link to the original ranking is here and it contains 15000 words, out of which I had filtered out 232 of them, since they are either symbols or Latin characters.
